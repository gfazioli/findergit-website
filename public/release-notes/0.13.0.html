FinderGit 0.13.0

Two new dashboards — and FinderGit now tells you the moment your work gets noticed.

🗂️ Overview — your whole workspace at a glance

FinderGit's new default view aggregates every repository under all your root folders into one dashboard, so you see what needs you before opening anything:

  • Stat cards across the whole workspace: repositories discovered, clean / modified / unpushed counts, total disk footprint, stars and forks, and critical Repo Trust findings.
  • A working-tree health ring — at a glance, how much of your work is clean vs. uncommitted or unpushed.
  • Needs attention — every repo that's dirty, ahead, behind its upstream, or carrying a security finding, sorted most-urgent-first. Click a row to jump to it.
  • Top starred and Largest on disk highlight lists.
  • Panels are drag-and-drop reorderable, each refreshes independently, and the whole view stays smooth across hundreds of repositories.

👤 Account — your GitHub profile, without a browser

A second global view turns FinderGit into a window onto your GitHub identity:

  • Profile header plus stat tiles: followers, stars, public and private repo counts, forks, pull requests (with merged count), issues, a year of commits, and organizations.
  • A full year of contributions, switchable between the classic heatmap and a line graph.
  • Your top repositories, languages, and organizations.

⭐ New-star alerts

Know the moment one of your repositories earns a star — by name, not just a number:

  • A pulsing (+) badge on the Account view's Stars tile, with a popover listing exactly which repos were starred — click through to open any of them.
  • Optional desktop notifications (off by default) under Settings → Git, with a configurable check interval.
  • One shared baseline means you're never alerted about the same star twice, and a relaunch won't re-announce stars earned while FinderGit was closed.

⚙️ Refreshed Settings

Every preference now sits on its own labelled tile with an inline help affordance — cleaner and more legible across all tabs.

🛠️ Fixes & polish

  • Clearer error messages when a GitHub profile can't be fetched.
  • Numerous stability and consistency fixes across the new dashboards.
